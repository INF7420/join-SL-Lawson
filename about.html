  • Education

Although I have strong interests in Youth Librarianship, both my interests and coursework covers a broad spectrum of Librarianship topics. I am an active member of the Student Chapter of the American Library Association.

Whether ordering books and supplies, speaking to a group of parents or a philanthropic organization, or planning, promoting, and implementing fun, exciting, and relevant p rograms, or anything in between, I love what I do!
